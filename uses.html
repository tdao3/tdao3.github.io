Different Uses

There are so many different uses for a toaster oven.
Some common uses include:

Toasting Bread
Heating Pizza
Toasting Bagels

Some more rare uses include:

Baking Cookies
Making Popcorn
Cooking Eggs

User Interaction

Depending on what you are making, the process used when operating the toaster oven would be different,
but the general idea should be the same. The steps are as followed

  1. Pull open the door handle and put your food/ingredients in
  2. Close the door
  3. Decide what mode you need for your food

    4. Are you heating up food?
    Are you baking?
    Are you broiling?
    Are you toasting?

  4. Turn the knob to the appropriate mode
  5. Choose the appropriate temperature and turn the dial
  6. The last step is to set the timer following the instruction near the dial
